### 案件数量示例：按时间段筛选查询出各地市案卷数量
```
select count(1) as ZB,
       '用地管理' as FLMC,
       '用地报批' as ZLMC,
       '本年度建设用地报批总数及总面积' as ZBFLMC,
       '' as BZ,
       substr(a.xzqhdm,0,4)|| '00' xzqhdm_s
from (select ywid,
                       xzqhdm,
                       to_char(jsyd_bpxx.PZRQ, 'yyyy-MM-dd HH:mm:ss') as PZRQ
                  from jsyd_bpxx
                 where 
                   ywxl = 'JSYDSP'
           and  (jsyd_bpxx.spjb = '08' or spjb = '09' or spjb = '01' or spjb = '02')
                   and jsyd_bpxx.xmalx not like '%QYAJ%'
                   and LENGTH(jsyd_bpxx.pzwh) >7
                   and jsyd_bpxx.pzrq is not null
                   and jsyd_bpxx.xmmc <> '新建项目'
                   and jsyd_bpxx.jbr <> '白云区报件'
                   and jsyd_bpxx.xmmc not like '%测试%') A
           where a.PZRQ >='2011-10-01' AND a.PZRQ <='2022-12-31'
           GROUP BY substr(a.xzqhdm,0,4)
```
