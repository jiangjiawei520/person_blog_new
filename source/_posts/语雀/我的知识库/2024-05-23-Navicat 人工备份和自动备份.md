数据备份本身主要是为了预防一些意外，例如：服务器或者个人电脑的硬件故障、人为的错误操作等情况。一旦发生意外，可以从备份机上恢复需要的数据。对企业而言，数据备份分为服务器备份和办公电脑备份两个方面。
有关于备份的能力，Navicat 工具提供“人工备份”和“自动备份”两种功能。

注意：记得设置备份路径，不然会默认备份到C盘下

### 人工备份

2. 再在主工具栏中点击备份。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/12484160/1698910114832-2f79820b-a15f-4cdd-a7c0-068e92db9ae6.png#averageHue=%23fcfbfb&clientId=u8f1a238a-6137-4&from=paste&id=u391aad8b&originHeight=820&originWidth=1380&originalType=url&ratio=1&rotation=0&showTitle=false&status=done&style=none&taskId=u4b82d386-47da-40ca-8e57-3093c594f1b&title=)
3. 然后在对象工具栏中点击“新建备份”。
![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/12484160/1698910114760-803e3205-ea33-4dee-9d9d-5c1d492a1a51.png#averageHue=%23fcfbfb&clientId=u8f1a238a-6137-4&from=paste&id=u414fbff5&originHeight=818&originWidth=1380&originalType=url&ratio=1&rotation=0&showTitle=false&status=done&style=none&taskId=ua4be01a1-34d1-4409-a485-b334025160d&title=)

### 自动备份
9. 若是希望可以对刚才的对象执行自动备份，请点击”自动运行“。
![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/12484160/1698910197892-de2d6259-0262-495e-8e4b-334bc56ee03c.png#averageHue=%23fcfbfb&clientId=u8f1a238a-6137-4&from=paste&id=u676233ae&originHeight=821&originWidth=1380&originalType=url&ratio=1&rotation=0&showTitle=false&status=done&style=none&taskId=ue6cd88b9-459e-496a-a737-0d0a07a49b3&title=)
10. 点击”新建批处理作业".
![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/12484160/1698910197919-9fbd6cab-3b4c-4bd3-b64c-6c1e43664eee.png#averageHue=%23fcfbfb&clientId=u8f1a238a-6137-4&from=paste&id=u0c4eb58d&originHeight=818&originWidth=1380&originalType=url&ratio=1&rotation=0&showTitle=false&status=done&style=none&taskId=uf8d9e734-4982-4b9d-a6e9-53ace543e5c&title=)
11. 然后选择“备份”，然后点击对于的database，在“可用的工作”中找到刚刚创建的配置文件并双击它。（此处是双击"b1")![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/12484160/1698910206057-2129ebae-3d44-48c0-a3d4-3e84bc886da5.png#averageHue=%23faf9f9&clientId=u8f1a238a-6137-4&from=paste&id=u214aca98&originHeight=821&originWidth=1380&originalType=url&ratio=1&rotation=0&showTitle=false&status=done&style=none&taskId=ufb01cbd8-8566-4b0c-b3d9-d19ed3a760b&title=)
12. 双击“b1”后，会在“已选择的工作”中显示此配置文件。（也可以在“可用的工作”中双击多个文件进行备份）
![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/12484160/1698910206062-1aed251c-0a03-4b46-b3af-f9f1c9840235.png#averageHue=%23faf8f7&clientId=u8f1a238a-6137-4&from=paste&id=u45f7a004&originHeight=817&originWidth=1380&originalType=url&ratio=1&rotation=0&showTitle=false&status=done&style=none&taskId=u2f6e7e22-f902-49c0-80e4-10b06c80d4c&title=)![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/12484160/1698910214958-e91eb4eb-e8fb-4123-8ba1-8ff3b7623b97.png#averageHue=%23f9f9f9&clientId=u8f1a238a-6137-4&from=paste&id=uba016238&originHeight=821&originWidth=1380&originalType=url&ratio=1&rotation=0&showTitle=false&status=done&style=none&taskId=u11b8b3a8-9511-49cc-b9f0-a4b28081ab6&title=)
13. 选择完需要备份的配置文件后，点击“保存”并命名配置文件。
![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/12484160/1698910214987-3fc3c1ac-80c7-4f32-9150-2181a99ae7f2.png#averageHue=%23f9f7f7&clientId=u8f1a238a-6137-4&from=paste&id=ue2e444dc&originHeight=818&originWidth=1380&originalType=url&ratio=1&rotation=0&showTitle=false&status=done&style=none&taskId=u796b58ad-680f-42b4-af7f-7d79f5afe60&title=)14. 保存完后，还可以设置备份时间表，点击“设置任务计划”（此选项只有点击保存后才会显示）。
![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/12484160/1698910223580-324eccd8-2260-462e-a0f3-cbe281837e2a.png#averageHue=%23f7f5f4&clientId=u8f1a238a-6137-4&from=paste&id=u31343666&originHeight=820&originWidth=1380&originalType=url&ratio=1&rotation=0&showTitle=false&status=done&style=none&taskId=u1e4142b0-180b-47a6-a8de-4347a646db1&title=)

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/12484160/1698910223508-89c6513d-3907-4f5c-956b-f6adf9791aab.png#averageHue=%23f8f7f6&clientId=u8f1a238a-6137-4&from=paste&id=u39a5d8aa&originHeight=814&originWidth=1380&originalType=url&ratio=1&rotation=0&showTitle=false&status=done&style=none&taskId=uf7fa66a8-0fbf-42dc-b64c-17da7e72bac&title=)
16. 设置需要自动备份的时间表，设置完后，点击“确定”。
![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/12484160/1698910231818-db388768-805a-4db2-8e9e-0b9e186defc7.png#averageHue=%23f4f2f2&clientId=u8f1a238a-6137-4&from=paste&id=u66728bb6&originHeight=817&originWidth=1380&originalType=url&ratio=1&rotation=0&showTitle=false&status=done&style=none&taskId=uaa161a6b-f105-49c7-9de6-dc3d78a8840&title=)
17. 然后，点击“开始”，可以进行一次备份。
![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/12484160/1698910231775-3ac7815b-4ec1-4bc4-a966-08750ee7c434.png#averageHue=%23faf9f8&clientId=u8f1a238a-6137-4&from=paste&id=u2c1bb3a0&originHeight=821&originWidth=1380&originalType=url&ratio=1&rotation=0&showTitle=false&status=done&style=none&taskId=uc2ea37b6-7348-4112-ac3f-1a0ba50ea3a&title=)
18. 点击“对象”，可以看到我们刚刚保存的自动运行的配置文件。
![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/12484160/1698910231756-312c895e-cd1f-4e3d-af35-cd8d281cef6e.png#averageHue=%23fcfbfb&clientId=u8f1a238a-6137-4&from=paste&id=u2824070a&originHeight=822&originWidth=1380&originalType=url&ratio=1&rotation=0&showTitle=false&status=done&style=none&taskId=ud026bb0b-4d1f-415d-8b33-86cdc936b68&title=)
