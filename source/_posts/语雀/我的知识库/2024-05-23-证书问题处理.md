证书问题：sun.security.validator.ValidatorException: PKIX path building failed: sun.security.provider.certpath.SunCertPathBuilderException: unable to find valid certification path to requested target、General SSLEngine problem问题
1、导出证书文件
2、导入服务器
1）需要安装有jdk
2）上传服务器
3）导入证书
4）输入默认密码changeit
5）回车确认，成功会返回Certificate was added to keystore
<!--more-->
6）重启微服务或者程序
cd ${JAVA_HOME}/jre/lib/security/
keytool -import -alias dcsj -keystore ${JAVA_HOME}/jre/lib/security/cacerts -file ${JAVA_HOME}/jre/lib/security/dcsj.crt

删除证书
keytool -delete -alias dcsj -keystore ${JAVA_HOME}/jre/lib/security/cacerts
输入默认密码changeit
