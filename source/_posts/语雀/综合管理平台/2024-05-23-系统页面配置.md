资源需要查询的主表，不用资源，不同主表
```plsql
select infor_resources_code from infor_resources_gl where catalog_id = #catalogId#

select infor_resources_code from infor_resources_gl where catalog_seq like '#catalogSeq#%'

select infor_resources_code from infor_resources_gl where system_id = '#systemId#'
```
